Industrial organisations have two technology estates that rarely speak to each other. The IT side — enterprise applications, cloud infrastructure, corporate systems — and the OT side: the operational technology that runs physical processes, monitors equipment, controls pipelines and plant. Different vendors, different standards, different operational rhythms, different risk tolerances.

Closing that gap is one of the more interesting problems in enterprise technology right now.
